Our Product

Coconut Fiber/Coir

Is a natural fiber extracted from coconut outer shell and used in products, such as; mat, door mat, paintbrush and bed. Coir is fibrous material found between the hard shell, coconut inner and outer layers. Other uses of brown coir (made from ripe coconut) is as sack coating and horticulture. White coir, harvested from raw coconut, used for fine brushes, rope and fishing nets. This product has the advantage of not sinking, so it can used in long period of time in the water.

Cocopeat

Is an organic growing media made from coconut coir powder. Cocopeat has the ability to absorb and store water which is quite high even better compared to soil. Utilization of Cocopeat has the potential to reduce topsoil use. Organic materials such as Cocopeat have pores and have crumb texture, so it is easier for water, air and sunlight to go through the soil.

Coconut charcoal briquette

Is the alternative fuel which mostly use for cook specially for baking food and also for Shisha pipe. Coconut charcoal briquettes produce more heat than coal briquettes

Virgin Coconut Oil

Virgin Coconut Oil is coconut oil which obtained by extraction or compression at a temperature not exceeding 60 degrees Celsius, so that the result of oil is clearer like water and also the nutrient content, the smell, and the taste of coconut is still good.

Semi Husked Coconut

Is an old coconut which fibers are peeled so that the shell is visible. These products are basic ingredients for food and beverages, cosmetics, etc.
